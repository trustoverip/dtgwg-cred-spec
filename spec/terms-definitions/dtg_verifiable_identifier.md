[[def: DTG verifiable identifier, DTG verifiable identifiers, VID, VIDs]]

~ A [verifiable identifier](https://glossary.trustoverip.org/#term:verifiable-identifier) (VID) used to identify a [[ref: DTG node]]. The DTG specifications use [decentralized identifiers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Decentralized_identifier) (DIDs) for verifiable identifiers. There are four types of DTG VIDs: [[ref: R-DIDs]], [[ref: M-DIDs]], [[ref: C-DIDs]], and [[ref: P-DIDs]].
