[[def: VTC member, VTC members]]

~ A person or any other type of [[ref: DTG node]] that has accepted a [[ref: VMC]] from a [[ref: VTC]]. If the new member is another VTC, it is identified using its [[ref: C-DID]]; otherwise the new member is identified using a [[ref: M-DID]]. A common pattern (depending on VTC governance) is for existing VTC members to invite new members by issuing them a [[ref: VTC invitation credential]].
