[[def: DTG credential, DTG credentials]]

~ A [[ref: VC]] defined by the [[ref: DTG Credentials Specification]] that is used to create or annotate a [trust relationship](https://glossary.trustoverip.org/#term:trust-relationship) in a [[ref: DTG]]. There are three subtypes of DTG credentials: [[ref: DTG edge credentials]], [[ref: DTG annotation credentials]], and [[ref: DTG invitation credentials]].
