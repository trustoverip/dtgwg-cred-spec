[[def: persona, personas]]

~ An [identity](https://glossary.trustoverip.org/#term:identity) controlled by the person it identifies (or their [delegate](https://glossary.trustoverip.org/#term:delegation)) in order to manage [correlation privacy](https://glossary.trustoverip.org/#term:correlation-privacy). In a [[ref: DTG]], a persona has a unique [verifiable identifier](https://glossary.trustoverip.org/#term:verifiable-identifier) called a [[ref: P-DID]] that may be shared using a [[ref: VPC]].

~ An [[ref: M-DID]] can be considered a specialized form of persona that is required for membership in a [[ref: VTC]]. By contrast, a [[ref: P-DID]] is a persona whose sharing is completely under the control of the asserting person.
