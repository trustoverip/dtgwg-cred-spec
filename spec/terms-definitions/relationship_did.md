[[def: R-DID, relationship DID, R-DIDs]]

~ A [decentralized identifier](https://glossary.trustoverip.org/#term:decentralized-identifier) (DID) used by one peer to identify itself to the other peer in a peer-to-peer [trust relationship](https://glossary.trustoverip.org/#term:trust-relationship) within a [[ref: DTG]]. R-DIDs are not intended to be used for any type of correlation beyond the scope of the two peers. Intentional correlation is the purpose of [[ref: M-DIDs]], [[ref: P-DIDs]], and [[ref: C-DIDs]].
