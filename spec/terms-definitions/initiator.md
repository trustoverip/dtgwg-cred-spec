[[def: initiator, initiators]]

~ A person or persons who initiate the establishment of a new [[ref: VTC]]. Depending on the policies governing the VTC, the initiator(s) will typically generate the [[ref: C-DID]] identifying the VTC, instantiate the [[ref: VTA]] representing the VTC, and invite the set of [[ref: CTAs]] who may then begin inviting other [[ref: VTC members]].
