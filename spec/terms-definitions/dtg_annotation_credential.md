[[def: DTG annotation credential, DTG annotation credentials]]

~ A type of [[ref: DTG credential]] that annotates an existing [[ref: DTG edge]] with additional assertions about the [trust relationship](https://glossary.trustoverip.org/#term:trust-relationship). The DTG Credentials Specification defines three subtypes of DTG annotation credentials: [[ref: VPCs]], [[ref: VECs]], and [[ref: VWCs]].
