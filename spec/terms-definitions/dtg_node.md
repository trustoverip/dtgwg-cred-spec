[[def: DTG node, DTG nodes]]

~ In a [[ref: DTG]], a DTG node represents an entity that participates in [trust relationships](https://glossary.trustoverip.org/#term:trust-relationship) with other DTG nodes. DTG node types include persons, devices, AI agents, and [[ref: VTCs]]. A DTG node is always identified by at least one [[ref: DTG verifiable identifier]] (VID) and can be interacted with via a [[ref: VTA]].
