[[def: VTN member, VTN members]]

~ A [[ref: VTC]] that has accepted a [[ref: VMC]] in a [[ref: VTN]]. In this VMC, the VTC member is identified using its [[ref: C-DID]]. A common pattern (depending on VTN governance) is for existing VTN members to invite new members by issuing them a [[ref: VTN invitation credential]].

~ VTN members are typically registered in one or more [trust registries](https://glossary.trustoverip.org/#term:trust-registry) serving the VTN.
