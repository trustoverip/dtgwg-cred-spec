[[def: VPC, verifiable persona credential, VPCs, verifiable persona credentials]]

~ A [[ref: DTG annotation credential]] that enables one party to a [[ref: DTG edge]] relationship to assert a [[ref: persona]] to the counterparty. The issuer of the VPC is identified with a [[ref: P-DID]]. The counterparty (holder) can now prove that the counterparty knows the issuer in the context of that persona. Persona DIDs enable individuals to control intentional correlation separately from their [[ref: M-DIDs]].
