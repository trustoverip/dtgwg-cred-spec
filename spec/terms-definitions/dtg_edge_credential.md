[[def: DTG edge credential, DTG edge credentials]]

~ A type of [[ref: DTG credential]] that identifies one direction of a bidirectional [trust relationship](https://glossary.trustoverip.org/#term:trust-relationship) in a [[ref: DTG]]. DTG edge credentials are issued in pairs, one in each direction, to form a [[ref: DTG edge]] connecting two [[ref: DTG nodes]]. There are two subtypes of DTG edge credentials: [[ref: VRCs]] and [[ref: VMCs]].
